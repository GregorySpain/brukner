Current Events:

Summer Art Exhibit:
“The Magic of Spring”

by Nora Steele

Runs through Sun., Sept. 18th

Nora is a Naturalist for Wahkeena Nature Preserve and uses her illustration and photography to educate about the natural world. Her amazing art will be on sale and ready for pick-up at the end of the show. Proceeds will support the Wildlife Ambassador program at BNC.

Featured Nature Shop Item:
Polymer Clay Jewelry

by Lilian Nichols

The artist of the month is Lilian Nichols, who works with polymer clay to make all sorts of jewelry — earrings, necklaces and other items.

For her, polymer clay is like three dimensional paints — a perfect combination of her first two loves — painting and ceramics. Always a nature lover, her art is inspired by colors, forms and patterns found in the natural world, sometimes with a touch of whimsy. She has been working in polymer clay for about thirty years, and also teaches, spreading the fun!

Jewelry prices range $15 — $45

*A percentage of all sales will be donated back to BNC
